Beginner Level Courses

Our beginner level courses are designed for learners who have little to no experience with the English language. These courses cover basic vocabulary, grammar, and sentence structures, and are designed to help learners develop their speaking and listening skills. Our beginner level courses are perfect for learners who want to start learning English from scratch.

Intermediate Level Courses

Our intermediate level courses are designed for learners who have some basic knowledge of English but need to improve their speaking and listening skills. These courses cover more advanced vocabulary, grammar, and sentence structures, and are designed to help learners develop their fluency in English. Our intermediate level courses are perfect for learners who want to improve their English skills for work or study.

Advanced Level Courses

Our advanced level courses are designed for learners who have a good command of the English language but need to refine their speaking and listening skills. These courses cover complex vocabulary, grammar, and sentence structures, and are designed to help learners achieve near-native fluency in English. Our advanced level courses are perfect for learners who want to improve their English skills for professional purposes.

Course Content

Our course is divided into several modules, each of which focuses on a different aspect of spoken English. The modules are as follows:

Pronunciation

This module covers the basics of English pronunciation, including vowel and consonant sounds, stress, and intonation. You will learn how to produce sounds correctly, and how to use stress and intonation to convey meaning.

Vocabulary

In this module, you will learn new words and phrases, and how to use them correctly in context. You will also learn strategies for expanding your vocabulary and remembering new words.

Grammar

This module covers the fundamental aspects of English grammar, including verb tenses, sentence structure, and parts of speech. You will learn how to use grammar correctly to communicate more effectively.

Conversation Skills

This module focuses on the skills needed for effective communication in English, including listening comprehension, speaking fluency, and the ability to engage in meaningful conversations.
